Our Mocktails

Discover our delicious range of handcrafted Mocktails.

Jamun Kala Khatta

A vibrant mocktail featuring the deep flavors of jamun (Indian blackberry), lemon, lime, and a medley of Indian masalas. This tangy and slightly spicy drink is a nostalgic favorite during festive and summer seasons.

Chilli Guava

This unique mocktail combines the sweetness of guava with a kick of chili, creating a bold and flavorful drink that leaves a lasting impression. Perfect for adventurous taste buds.

Aam Panna

A refreshing mocktail made from raw mangoes, Indian spices, and masalas. Aam Panna is a traditional summer drink known for its cooling and tangy-sweet flavors, making it a must-have for hot days.

Kiwi Berry Mocktail

The Kiwi Berry Mocktail is a tropical medley of kiwi, mixed berries, lemon, and lime, offering a burst of tart and sweet flavors. Rich in antioxidants and vitamin C, this drink supports immunity and hydration. A perfect choice for poolside relaxation or a fruity refreshment.
